1. Where is my nearest McDonald’s ® Restaurant?

You can find your nearest McDonald’s restaurant by visiting www.mcdonalds.co.nz/find-us/restaurants

2. How long does the promotion run?

The promotion starts at 12:00am NZDT on 25 th January 2017 and ends 11:59pm NZDT on 28 th February 2017.

3. How do I enter the promotion?

  1. Purchase any Combo Meal, Hunger Buster ® Meal or Macca’s ® ShareBox ® from a participating McDonald’s restaurant during the promotional period;

  2. Obtain one (1) Game Card for any Combo Meal or Hunger Buster ® Meal purchased OR two (2) Game Cards for any Macca’s ® ShareBox ® or Mates Hunger Buster ® purchased;

  3. Scratch the code panel on the Game Card to reveal:

    4. i.

    the unique Game Card code (Unique Code).

    ii.

    the instant win prize message (Instant Win Prize Message). The Instant Win Prize Message may reveal that the Game Card entitles the Entrant to claim an Instant Win Food Prize, or may reveal that the Game Card is not an instant win Game Card

  4. Keep your McDonald’s receipt and Game card(s). If you have an Instant Win on your Game Card, you will need to hand your Game Card over to claim your Instant Win Food Item. In this instance, we recommend that you keep your receipt and take a picture of your Game Card with your smart phone, showing your Unique Code.

  5. Enter your Unique Code on this website to go into the Daily Draw or claim your Instant Win Food Prize in store in the event of a win.

  6. All entries for each day will go in to the applicable daily draw.

4. What are the prizes?

The following prizes are being given away throughout the promotion:

Daily Prize Draw Prizes

  1. $1,000 Cash

  2. 20 x $50 Prezzy ® Cards

  3. 30 x $20 Macca’s ® Vouchers


Instant Win Food Prizes

  1. Big Mac ® burger

  2. Cheeseburger

  3. Regular sundae

  4. Small fries

See the Prize Details section of this website for more details.

5. What are Game Cards?

Upon purchasing any Combo Meal or Hunger Buster® Meal the Entrant will receive one official Game Card from the staff at the participating McDonald’s restaurant. Upon purchasing a Mates Hunger Buster® or Macca’s® ShareBox®, the Entrant will receive two official Game Cards.

Each Game Card contains a Scratch Panel that reveals a Unique Code and an Instant Win Prize Message once scratched.

The Unique Code entitles the Entrant to enter the Daily Draw at www.monthofmoolah.co.nz.

The Instant Win Prize Message may reveal that the Game Card entitles the Entrant to claim an Instant Win Food Prize, or may reveal that the Game Card is not an instant win Game Card.

6. Where do I obtain a Game Card?

Game Cards are issued with the purchase of any Combo, Hunger Buster ® Meal or Macca’s ® ShareBox ® at participating McDonald’s restaurants.

7. Can I enter more than once?

Yes, however entries are limited to 2 per person, per day.

8. How will I be notified if I’m a winner?

$1,000 Cash prize winners will be contacted by phone and in writing.


$50 Prezzy ® card and $20 Macca’s voucher prize winners will be notified via email. Included in the winner’s communication will be a Prize Claim Code and instructions on how to claim your prize.

9. Who can enter the promotion?

Entry into the promotion is only open to New Zealand residents aged 14 years or older (exclusions apply – see full terms and conditions). All entries of Entrants who are under the age of 18 years and enter the promotion without disclosing (or attempting to conceal) their age (as and where required) and/or do not provide the required parental consent (as and where required) will be deemed invalid.

10. I am under 18 years of age, can I still win a prize?

Yes, if any prize winner is aged under 18 years but older than 14 years, the prize will be awarded to the parent or legal guardian who consented to the winner entering the promotion, at the discretion of the Promoter.

11. Why do I need to keep my Game Card after I’ve entered the promotion?

Under the Terms and Conditions all prize winners may be required to produce their Game Card for validation purposes, so make sure you keep your Game Card in a safe place! If you win an Instant Win Food Prize on your Game Card, you will need to hand it over to restaurant crew to claim your item. In this instance, we recommend you keep your receipt and take a photo of your game card, showing your Unique Code, for prize validation purposes.

12. I have claimed my prize, but I haven’t received it yet?

Delivery of prizes may take up to 28 days from validation. Please contact our Customer Services Team on 0800 383 732 if you still haven’t received your prize after 28 days from the date you claimed your prize.
